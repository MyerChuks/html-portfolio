My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Shot Caller

A movie about a once-successful business man fresh out of prison is forced to orchestrate a brazen crime or risk his family.

The Wolf of Wall Street

Leonardo DiCaprio is the reason I love this movie so much and his unbelievable oscar winning performance, this movie streamlines how a broke hungry-for-sucess trader uses his resources well to become wealthy which is short lived due to illegal activity.

Donnie Brasco

Another fantastic film that showcases the Italian mafia in ways never seen before. An undercover FBI agent infiltrates a New York Mafia family and develops a close friendship with a mob hitman, putting his job and life on the line.
